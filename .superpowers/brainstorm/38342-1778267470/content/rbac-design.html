Approaches for RBAC Enforcement

1

Shared Middleware

A single requireRole(roles...) middleware that checks the JWT/Session for the user's role before allowing access to routes.

2

Path-based Isolation

Hard isolation: /admin/* routes are handled by a completely different mux group with its own stricter security policies.
